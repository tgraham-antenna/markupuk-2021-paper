Hints for conference paper in DocBook

Author information

author

Provide information about you, your activity, things you accomplished, etc.

Article information

keywordset

Specify one or more keywords relevant for your paper

abstract

Describe your paper in a few paragraphs.

Sections information

section

You can organize your article in multiple sections. It is recommended to start with an "Introduction" section and end with a "Conclusion" section followed by bibliographic references.

There are a few actions available that allow you to add a new top level section or to promote or demote a section like changing between level 1 and level 2 sections, for example.

bibliography

You should finish your article with references. You can easily cite references from you article using the citation element.
